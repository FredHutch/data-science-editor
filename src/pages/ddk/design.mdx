## Overview

The hardware design guidelines for Jacdac are organized into four sections:
- [Electrical](./electrical)
- [Electro-mechanical](./electro-mechanical)
- [Mechanical](./mechanical)

## EC30 module form factor

If you are considering creating a Jacdac module, we recommend using the [EC30 module form factor](./ec30).
